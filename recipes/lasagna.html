Lasagna

Description

It is an Italian dish made of stacked layers of this flat pasta alternating with fillings such as ragù (a meat sauce or tomato sauce) and other vegetables, cheese (which may include ricotta and parmesan), and seasonings and spices such as garlic, oregano and basil.[1]

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all three together.
  2. Serve Hot
